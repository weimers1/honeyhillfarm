About Us

Welcome to HoneyHill Farm, located in Livonia, New York, a hamlet in the Finger Lakes Region of Upstate New York. Our farm is dedicated to producing high-quality organic 100% grass-fed and grass-finished black Angus beef through sustainable grazing and farming practices. We are open to customers and visitors by appointment -- please contact us to schedule a visit. Learn more about our story and our commitment to quality.
