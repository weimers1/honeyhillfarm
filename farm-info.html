About Our Farm

Welcome to HoneyHill Farm, located in the picturesque town of Livonia, New York. Our farm is dedicated to producing the highest quality beef through sustainable and ethical farming practices. We believe in treating our animals with the utmost care and providing them with a natural and stress-free environment. We are pleased to provide a tour of our entire operation (and a hayride!) to introduce people to the way all cattle should be raised!

Our Farming Practices

At HoneyHill Farm, we employ a variety of sustainable farming techniques to ensure the health and well-being of our cattle. Some of our key practices include:

  • Managed-Grazing Method: We rotate our cattle through different pastures and move our cattle three times per day to prevent overgrazing and promote soil health. Our managed-grazing ensures pastures rebound quickly, mimicking the natural grazing patterns of North American bison.
  • Organic Feed: Our cattle are fed a diet of 100% organic, non-GMO grasses. We raise championship Black Angus Beef Cattle with superior genetics sourced from the WYE Plantation for 100% grass-fed Angus.
  • Natural Water Sources: We provide our cattle with access to fresh, clean water from natural springs on our property.
  • Natural Weed Management: By understanding the life cycle of weeds, we manage them naturally without poisons or herbicides.
  • No Chemicals Ever: We never use any antibiotics on our cattle as we ensure they are always healthy and protected. We have never applied any herbicides, pesticides, or artificial substances to our land since 1978.
  • Dung Beetle Integration: We use no substances that would harm beneficial insects like dung beetles, allowing them to thrive. Dung beetles on our farm quickly remove manure underground, reducing pests and naturally fertilizing our pastures.
  • Minimal Motorized Equipment: The only motorized equipment used on our farm is a tractor with a mower, promoting a natural environment.
  • Clean and Safe Environment: Our farm is clean, neat, and orderly, ensuring that food is raised in a safe environment.
  • Dry-Aged Beef: All of our beef is dry-aged for two full weeks, then cut and vacuum sealed by our professional butcher.

Grass-Fed Beef

Our beef is 100% grass-fed and grass-finished, which means our cattle graze on our lush, green pastures for the entire season. This method not only provides our animals with a natural diet but also results in beef that is higher in healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals. Grass-fed beef is known for its rich flavor and superior nutritional profile.

Quality Assurance

We are committed to producing beef of the highest quality. Our farming practices are designed to maintain the health and well-being of our cattle, which translates into better quality meat for you. Our commitment to quality is backed by studies conducted by Cornell University, which have consistently shown the superiority of our beef.
